Artist Residency

Artist Run is inviting proposals for a series of one month residencies. Artists should be practicing in their field and be able to propose a project idea with the intention to present one open studio event for the local community plus an exhibition at the end of the residency period.


Successful recipients will receive shared access to a large studio space, throughout the program artists will receive specialised support. We currently can't offer accommodation, applicants should have means to reach the studio in South London.


All practising artists are eligible to apply. All proposals will be assessed and we endeavour to feedback to each applicant within two months. Please include below examples of most recent work.
